# Family Emergency Preparedness - Belgium 🇧🇪

A comprehensive, interactive emergency preparedness checklist specifically designed for families in Belgium.

## Features

- **Interactive Form** - Fill out directly in browser, auto-saves progress
- **Belgium-Specific** - Covers Belgian emergency scenarios (flooding, nuclear plants, etc.)
- **Print-Friendly** - Clean print stylesheet for physical copies
- **Minimal Design** - Clean, distraction-free interface
- **12 Comprehensive Sections** - From rally points to action items

## Sections

1. Emergency Scenarios (flooding, nuclear, weather, etc.)
2. Rally Points & Evacuation Routes
3. "What If" Decision Trees
4. Communication Plan
5. Supplies & Resources
6. Special Needs (medical, pets)
7. Financial Preparedness
8. Home Security & Evacuation
9. Official Resources (BE-Alert, emergency numbers)
10. Training & Practice
11. Action Items
12. Family Agreement

## Usage

1. Open the checklist in your browser
2. Fill out all sections with your family
3. Progress saves automatically to your browser
4. Print or save as PDF when complete
5. Review and update every 6 months

## Emergency Numbers Belgium

- **112** - General Emergency
- **101** - Police
- **100** - Fire Department
- **070 245 245** - Poison Control
- **116 000** - Missing Children
